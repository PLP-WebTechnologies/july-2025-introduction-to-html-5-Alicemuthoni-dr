Mental Health Among Youth(13-25)

SectionsIntroduction|Causes|Effects|Support

Introduction

Mental health is a crucial aspect of overall well-being,especially for young people navigating school,relationships and self identity

Common Causes

  • Academic pressure
  • Social media influence
  • Family and peer relationships
  • Substance abuse
  • Economic hardship

Effects of Poor Mental Health

If unaddressed,mental health issues can lead to deppression,anxiety,substance abuse and in several cases,suicidal thoughts

How to Support Youth

  1. Promote open conversations
  2. Encourage proffesional help
  3. Provide safe spaces for expression
  4. Educate about coping mechanisms
